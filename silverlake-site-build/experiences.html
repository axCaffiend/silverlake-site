The year is 2277 in the city of Silver Lake, a successful space mining outpost built in the crater an asteroid left 500 years ago on planet Cascade - 3013 light years from Earth. With many strange places to explore and plenty of adventures to have, no two visits will be the same!

ADVENTURES

Cloud Punk

Discover the city and its inhabitants through our adventure themed rides! Join the story and let Silver Lake's colourful characters take you on an awe-inspiring journey.

Space Miners

Recruits, get ready for a fantastic journey deep underground! Silver Lake city is running out of energy and the last fossil fuels will soon be exhausted. Commander Sedgewick sends you on a mission down The Mines to find the Energy Crystal, a great source of power. Find it and take it back to the ship so we can keep the our city with its energy. Will you succeed in your mission?

Silver Falls

The falls which give the city its name, look closely and you might just see a rainbow fish…head to Silver Falls for some fun for all the family with a race down our four lane super slide!

The Bifrost Incident

Are you brave enough to come face to face with the Void as it rises from the darkness of empty space? Take your one way ticket on a horrifying train journey into the chilling depths of the unknown. With intense live action and thrilling physical effects, the grasp of the Void awaits on this eldritch dark ride. Sinister surprises lie in every detail of this unique theme park ride.

Hover Race

Experience a fun, fast, first-time rollercoaster with a trip onto the Hover Racing Track! Now a firm family favourite at the park, Flying Fish is the perfect ride for young thrill seekers to earn their adrenaline apprenticeship.

THE LABS

Escape

You've been captured by The Labs for their mysterious experiments - something to do with 'gene splicing' and 'cyborg betas'. Either way that doesn't sound good. This VR escape game mixes VR and a full physical environment to create a larger-than-life adventure that would be far too dangerous to live in real life.

Activate

Play arcade-style games by actually jumping, climbing, and dodging. You and your group of 3-5 will move freely between eleven interactive rooms, choosing games that challenge your agility, problem solving, and speed. Dive through lasers. Climb glowing walls. Only the best can conquer them all. Think you have what it takes?

Play

Try out the next generation entertainment system, courtesy of The Labs… Simply select your game and prepare to enjoy a truly unique immersive experience with your group. With motion tracking, projection mapping, touch screens and surround sound - these Immersive Gameboxes will be like nothing you have experienced before! Perfect for adult groups, and families with children aged 5+.

The Arcades

Win

A huge area with the biggest games including deluxe cabinets you can play and ride. Wall-to-wall with classic arcade machines, pinball and modern games – there’s something for everyone!
